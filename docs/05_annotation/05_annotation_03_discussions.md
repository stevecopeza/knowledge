This document defines **Discussions** as structured, non-social analytical threads attached to knowledge.

---

## 1. Purpose

Discussions support:
- Collaborative analysis
- Multi-perspective reasoning
- Long-form thought capture

They are not social feeds.

---

## 2. Structure

A Discussion consists of:
- A root topic
- Ordered contributions
- Explicit references to Knowledge Objects or text

---

## 3. Referencing Rules

Discussion contributions may reference:
- Versions
- Forks
- Highlights
- Notes

References are explicit and immutable.

---

## 4. Lifecycle

States:
- Open
- Closed
- Archived

Rules:
- Closed discussions remain readable
- Archived discussions are inactive

---

## 5. Permissions

- Create discussion: Editor+
- Contribute: permission-scoped
- Close discussion: Reviewer+

---

## Closing Note

Discussions are analytical records. Engagement metrics and social dynamics are explicitly excluded.
